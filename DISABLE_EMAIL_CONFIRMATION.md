# Disable Email Confirmation in Supabase

To disable email confirmation requirement:

1. Go to your Supabase Dashboard: https://supabase.com/dashboard
2. Select your project: `frzhzilfagwzwzbnrnbg`
3. Navigate to Authentication > Settings
4. Under "User Signups" section, toggle OFF "Enable email confirmations"
5. Save the changes

This will allow users to log in immediately after signing up without needing to confirm their email.
